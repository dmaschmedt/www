Randy Terwilliger

Randy joined Maschmedt & Associates in December of 2019. His first twelve years in the industry were spent in field sales at a large outdoor sports distributor. His experience in the hunting, fishing, marine, and camping categories has prepared him well for his current role as Sales Associate. He is an avid outdoorsman and loves all the Midwest has to offer in outdoor sports.

Randy lives in the Kansas City area with his wife Emily and their four daughters: Mackenzie, Alexis, Brynlee, and Rowyn. He loves spending time with his family and always looks forward to their adventures together.
