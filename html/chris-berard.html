Chris Berard

Chris joined Maschmedt & Associates in April of 2023. Although this is his first sales position in the outdoor industry, he comes with a lifetime of experience in the outdoors. He has lived in South Louisiana all of his life where he enjoys his favorite past times. If he is not fishing or hunting with his son Easton, you can find him at the baseball field where he coaches his young travel ball team. His competitive nature has always driven him to be successful in his hobbies and previous employments.

Chris takes a special interest in archery and enjoys hunting whitetail deer on public lands throughout the state of Louisiana. If he has free time during the Spring and Summer, he and his son Easton enjoy fishing local bass tournaments in the Atchafalaya Basin where he gained his strong passion for the outdoors.

Territory: Louisiana, So. Arkansas, E. Texas
