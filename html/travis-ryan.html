Travis Ryan

Travis has been with Maschmedt & Associates since 2018. Born and raised in Utah, his passion for the outdoors, whether hunting, fishing, camping, hiking or shooting, is what brought him into the Outdoor Sporting Goods industry. Travis relocated to Montana for his position with Maschmedt & Associates and it was a welcomed opportunity that he’s been waiting for. 

Prior to coming on with Maschmedt & Associates, he worked for Barnes Bullets which involved not only bullets, but many of the Remington Outdoor Company products.
