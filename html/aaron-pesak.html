Aaron Pesak

Aaron joined Maschmedt & Associates in June of 2023 and comes with a lifetime of outdoor experience. Aarons enjoys learning from others and sharing his experiences in the community through different organizations, such as local game management associations, youth organizations, Hunt for the Hungry, veteran and guided hunting, as well as additional organizations. His drive for finding and developing high quality products and ability to network with assorted retailers and professionals has proven him successful in his endeavors.

Aaron takes a special interest in archery and looks forward to camping high in the mountains while tracking elk, scouting and hunting whitetail deer near his hometown, Weimar Tx., and other states, fishing the nearby coastal waters, as well as helping to control feral hog numbers.
