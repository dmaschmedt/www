Kelly Bachelder

One of the most recent additions to the Maschmedt team, Kelly has been with the company since April of 2019. Prior to joining the Maschmedt team, she worked for Gerber Gear for over 7 years in multiple roles from customer service, to technical representative, and sales manager for the Northwest and Canada. During her time at Gerber, Kelly got the chance to work directly with many of the Maschmedt team members. This helped solidify that she would be a good fit for both parties. Some of the larger accounts she covered include BiMart, Zulily, Sportsman’s Warehouse, Overstock.com, Wholesale Sports, and United Farmers Coop.

Kelly lives on 5 acres with her husband of 1-year Matt, and his two teenage daughters. They enjoy camping, fishing, hiking, and visiting their family cabin in Wyoming.
