Paul Spencer

Paul is a new member of the Maschmedt team but comes with nearly 30 years of industry experience, starting as a small sporting goods business owner, a buyer for a large Sporting goods chain store and recently as a National Account manager for a large Sporting goods industry leading vendor. Paul Spencer has a love of this industry and this outdoors lifestyle as an avid fisherman, hunter, camper and all-around outdoorsman with his family. Living in Utah for most of his 49 years of life, he knows the western area and lifestyle and appreciates all aspects and challenges within this region.

Paul lives in the Salt Lake City area with his wife Angie of 30 years and for them family both immediate and extended is everything, but most importantly their 3 children. Today Paul manages the Utah, Colorado and New Mexico territories for Maschmedt with a focus on the Spring and Summer lines.
