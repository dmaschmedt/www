David O’Donnell

David started with Maschmedt & Associates in February of 2018. Living in south central Louisiana, he has close access to some of the world’s most premier fishing and duck hunting habitats, along with the retailers that supply the gear to the people that take part in those adventures. David came to us from his position managing a sporting goods retail store where he learned many ins and outs of the industry. He was given the award of Rookie of the Year within our company and aided his team of 4 in becoming Daiwa’s Rep Group of the Year, their first year in the TALO territory.

His hobbies include fishing for redfish, largemouth, and offshore pelagic, along with bowhunting whitetails and duck hunting the swamps of southern Louisiana.

Territory: LA, Southern AR, East TX
